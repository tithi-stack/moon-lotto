# Lottario Game Rules & Prize Structure

## Game Overview
**Lottario** is an Ontario-only lottery game known for its Early Bird draw and growing Main Jackpot.
- **Draw Day**: Saturday
- **Draw Time**: Shortly after 10:30 PM ET
- **Sales Cutoff**: 10:30 PM ET on Saturday night
- **Cost**: $1.00 per play (includes 2 selections of 6 numbers)

## How to Play
1.  **Selection**: You choose **6 numbers** from 1 to 45.
2.  **Double Play**: For every $1 play, you get TWO sets of numbers.
    -   Set 1: You pick (or Quick Pick).
    -   Set 2: Automatically generated Quick Pick.
    -   *Both sets are eligible for prizes.*

---

## 1. Main Draw
Six numbers and one Bonus number are drawn from 1 to 45.

### Prize Structure (Main Draw)

| Match | Prize | Prize Type | Odds (per $1 play) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **6/6** | **Jackpot** (Min $250K) | Share of Pool (51% + Rollover) | 1 in 4,072,530 |
| **5/6 + Bonus** | **$10,000.00** | Share of Pool | 1 in 678,755 |
| **5/6** | **$500.00** | Share of Pool | 1 in 17,862 |
| **4/6 + Bonus** | **$30.00** | Share of Pool | 1 in 7,145 |
| **4/6** | **$10.00** | Share of Pool | 1 in 386 |
| **3/6 + Bonus** | **$5.00** | Share of Pool | 1 in 290 |
| **3/6** | **$4.00** | Share of Pool | 1 in 24.1 |
| **0/6 + Bonus** | **Free Play** | Fixed (Lottario Free Play) | 1 in 8.1 |

**Overall Odds of winning any Main Draw prize**: 1 in 5.8

*Note: While many lower-tier prizes are listed with dollar amounts (e.g., $10, $5, $4), they are technically calculated as shares of the prize pool. However, historically they often settle near these fixed targets unless the pool is unusually low.*

---

## 2. Early Bird Draw
Tickets purchased **before Friday at 11:59:59 PM ET** are eligible for the Early Bird draw.
- **Prize**: Share of the **$50,000** Early Bird Pot.
- **Mechanics**: 4 numbers are drawn from 1 to 45. You must match all 4 numbers on one of your lines to win.
- **Odds**: 1 in 4,967.

---

## Evaluation & Tracking Notes
- **Lottario is unique** because players get 2 lines for $1.
- The **Early Bird** is a separate check that applies to eligible tickets (bought before Friday midnight).
- **Jackpot**: Starts at $250,000 and grows until won. It is shared if there are multiple winners.
- **0/6 + Bonus**: This is a high-frequency prize (1 in 8.1) that rewards a Free Play.

## Historical Context
- Lottario was Ontario's first online terminal lotto game, launched in 1978.
- It remains an Ontario exclusive.
